# 🐷 Pig Dice Game

A two player game where players take turns rolling, banking points, and racing to reach the selected target score first.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🎮 **Two-player turn-based gameplay**  
  Play locally as Player 1 vs Player 2

- 🎯 **Selectable target score**  
  Choose to play to **50** or **100** points

- 🎲 **Dice rolling mechanics**  
  Roll to build up your turn score, but rolling a **1** ends your turn and loses the unbanked points

- 🏦 **Banking system**  
  Bank your current turn score to safely add it to your total

- 🏆 **Win state feedback**  
  Clear winner message when a player reaches the target score

- 🌙 **Dark mode toggle**  
  Switch between light and dark themes

- 🔊 **Sound effects**
  Sound toggle for gameplay feedback

- ⌨️ **Keyboard shortcuts**
  Play actions can also be triggered with keyboard input
